### Project context
HUFT is Looking for help in order to leverage AI for their marketing, reporting and planning. The main sponsor for the project is Nishad, who is the Chief Business Officer with three team members under him. Nishad's main ask is that today the team struggles with all the different data sources available and the complexity of reporting and analysis which is making planning discussions and decisions suboptimal. The bottleneck is a combination of a lot of time being taken for reporting, which means there is less time of actually doing the analysis required. There are possibly multiple ways in which we could help a project like this. One is making reporting itself easier where multiple data sources are connected in order to generate a report which the team doesn't have to manually do. The next step is AI driven recommendations on top of standardized reporting outputs. The challenge there is that there are multiple goals the team is pursuing and often the goals are not mutually exclusive. There is a lot of art towards how these goals are arrived at. For instance there is short term ROAS goals at the brand level and there is long term brand building and awareness goals as well. So in terms of a decision making framework the team might be okay with lower ROAS because it works towards longer term brand building.
